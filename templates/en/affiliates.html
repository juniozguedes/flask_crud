Business Models

All business models you can work with Afilio.

Affiliates can choose.

  • Double click

    Your commission is accounted for every time a qualified traffic is generated, a user with real interest in the product.

  • CPL – Cost per lead

    It is filling out a register used to increase those interested by the product or service offered by an advertiser.

  • CPA – Cost per acquisition or action

    Every time a visitor makes a purchase, you are commissioned. The commission is a percentage of the sale or a fixed amount.

  • CPM – Cost per thousand impressions

    Every 1000 times that the banner appears on the screen of your site visitors, you are commissioned. They aim to generate traffic for the strengthening or launch of a brand.

  • CPC – Cost per clic

    With each click done on a banner within your site, you will be commissioned. Very common in branding and traffic campaigns.
